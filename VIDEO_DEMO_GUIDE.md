## ðŸŽ¬ PRODUCTION GUIDELINES

### Equipment Needed

**Minimum Setup:**
- Smartphone with good camera (iPhone 11+, Samsung S20+)
- Tripod or stable surface
- Good lighting (natural or ring light)
- Quiet environment
- Screen recording software (OBS, Loom, or built-in)

**Professional Setup:**
- DSLR or mirrorless camera
- External microphone (Rode VideoMic, Blue Yeti)
- Softbox lighting (2-3 lights)
- Green screen (optional)
- Professional editing software (Adobe Premiere, Final Cut)

---

### Recording Tips

**Video Quality:**
- Resolution: 1080p minimum (4K preferred)
- Frame rate: 30fps or 60fps
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (widescreen)

**Audio Quality:**
- Use external microphone if possible
- Record in quiet environment
- Avoid echo (use soft furnishings)
- Test audio levels before recording
- Speak clearly and at moderate pace

**Lighting:**
- Face the light source
- Avoid harsh shadows
- Use 3-point lighting if possible
- Natural light is great (avoid direct sun)
- Consistent lighting throughout

**Framing:**
- Rule of thirds
- Eye level camera
- Clean background
- Proper headroom
- Stable camera (no shaking)

---

### Screen Recording

**Software Options:**
- **Loom** (easiest, free tier available)
- **OBS Studio** (free, professional)
- **Camtasia** (paid, easy editing)
- **ScreenFlow** (Mac, professional)
- **Built-in** (Windows Game Bar, Mac QuickTime)

**Recording Settings:**
- Full HD (1920x1080)
- 30fps minimum
- Show cursor
- Highlight clicks
- Hide notifications
- Clean desktop

**Best Practices:**
- Practice before recording
- Use smooth mouse movements
- Pause between sections
- Record in segments (easier to edit)
- Have script visible
- Zoom in on important details

---

### Editing Guidelines

**Structure:**
1. Hook (first 5 seconds)
2. Introduction (10-15 seconds)
3. Main content (1-2 minutes)
4. Call to action (5-10 seconds)
5. Branding (last 3 seconds)

**Editing Tips:**
- Cut dead air and mistakes
- Add text overlays for key points
- Use transitions sparingly
- Add background music (low volume)
- Include captions/subtitles
- Add logo/branding
- Export in high quality

**Music:**
- Use royalty-free music (Epidemic Sound, Artlist)
- Keep volume low (background only)
- Match mood to content
- Fade in/out smoothly
