## ‚ùì COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### Q: "Why Uzbekistan?"
**A:** "Uzbekistan has a rapidly growing e-commerce market ($2B+, 40% YoY growth) with limited competition. We have local market expertise, language support, and relationships with all major marketplaces. Once we dominate here, we'll expand to Central Asia."

### Q: "What about competition?"
**A:** "There are fulfillment providers and marketplace tools, but no one offers both in a single AI-powered platform. Our full-stack approach and superior unit economics give us a significant advantage."

### Q: "How do you acquire customers?"
**A:** "Three channels: 1) 5-tier referral system (built-in viral growth), 2) Content marketing (SEO, social media), 3) Direct sales to top sellers. Our CAC decreases from $1,140 to $245 as referrals kick in."

### Q: "What if AI costs increase?"
**A:** "We've built fallback modes that work without AI. Plus, we're training our own models to reduce dependency on third-party APIs. AI is an enhancement, not a requirement."

### Q: "How do you scale fulfillment?"
**A:** "70% of operations are automated. For physical fulfillment, we partner with existing warehouses and use our software to manage operations. We're asset-light and highly scalable."

### Q: "What's your moat?"
**A:** "Four moats: 1) Technology - 41K+ lines of production code, 2) Network effects - referral system, 3) Data - marketplace intelligence, 4) Local expertise - relationships and language."

### Q: "When will you be profitable?"
**A:** "We break even at 10 partners (Month 2) and become profitable at 15+ partners (Month 3). With your investment, we'll be profitable within 90 days."

### Q: "What's the exit strategy?"
**A:** "Three options: 1) Strategic acquisition by e-commerce platform ($50-100M, 3-5 years), 2) Regional expansion + IPO ($200M+, 5-7 years), 3) Merger with logistics company ($75-150M, 4-6 years)."

### Q: "How big is the team?"
**A:** "Currently solo technical founder with production-ready platform. Post-investment, we'll hire CTO, Sales Lead, and Customer Success within 3 months. Full team of 10 by end of Year 1."

### Q: "What are the biggest risks?"
**A:** "Three risks: 1) AI costs - mitigated with fallback modes, 2) Competition - first-mover advantage, 3) Scaling ops - automated workflows. All have clear mitigation strategies."
