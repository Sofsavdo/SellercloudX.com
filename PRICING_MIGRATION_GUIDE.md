## üìã Executive Summary

BiznesYordam.uz is transitioning from a **profit-based commission model** to a **revenue-based subscription + commission model**. This change provides:

- ‚úÖ **Predictable monthly costs** with fixed subscription fees
- ‚úÖ **Transparent pricing** based on revenue, not profit
- ‚úÖ **Lower commission rates** (10-25% vs. previous 15-30%)
- ‚úÖ **Better scalability** as your business grows

---

## üîÑ What's Changing?

### Old Model (v2.x)
```
Commission = Profit √ó Commission Rate (15-30%)
- Variable commission based on profit margins
- No fixed monthly fee
- Commission rate: 15-30% of profit
```

### New Model (v3.0.0)
```
Total Cost = Monthly Subscription + (Revenue √ó Commission Rate)
- Fixed monthly subscription fee
- Commission based on revenue (10-25%)
- Predictable, transparent pricing
```

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Why is the model changing?
**A**: The new model provides more predictable costs, aligns with industry standards, and allows us to invest in better features and support.

### Q: Will my costs increase?
**A**: It depends on your profit margins. High-margin businesses may see lower costs, while low-margin businesses may see higher costs. Use our calculator to estimate.

### Q: Can I stay on the old model?
**A**: No, all partners must migrate by January 1, 2026. However, we offer a 30-day grace period for tier adjustments.

### Q: What if I can't afford the new pricing?
**A**: Contact your account manager to discuss options. We may offer temporary discounts or payment plans for qualifying partners.

### Q: How is commission calculated now?
**A**: Commission is calculated as a percentage of your **total revenue** (not profit). This is more transparent and easier to predict.

### Q: What happens to my existing contracts?
**A**: All existing contracts will be updated to reflect the new pricing model. You'll receive updated contract documents via email.

### Q: Can I change tiers later?
**A**: Yes, you can request tier upgrades anytime. Downgrades are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

### Q: Are there any hidden fees?
**A**: No. The only costs are the monthly subscription fee and the commission on your revenue. All other features are included in your tier.
